Pantai Mangrove

Mangrove forests are a very clear source for maintaining aquatic ecosystems between the sea, coast and land.
In addition, the benefits of mangrove forests will also help humans in getting the most comfortable climate
and weather to prevent natural disasters. Among the products that can be produced from processing
with mangrove raw materials are tofu, tempeh, lunkhead, candy, chips, various crackers and even
composting fermentation to soap. In Mangrove Beach, we can catch some crabs, play ATV, and
also planting mangrove trees
